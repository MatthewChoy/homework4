Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score with the given number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal. Good Luck.
